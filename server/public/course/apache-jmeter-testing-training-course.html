Capstone Projects
By the time you complete from our Jmeter Live training, you will get hands-on experience on multiple real projects to impress top tech recruiters with and land the job you want. Here’s a peek at some of the projects:
  • Salesforce CRM Development Platform

    Salesforce is the world’s #1 customer relationship management (CRM) platform. Salesforce platform help enterprises with marketing, sales, commerce, service and IT teams work as one from anywhere. Workiy provide test environment for automating about 40+ functional tests to have real-time experience.

  • ServiceNow ITSM Development Platform

    ServiceNow is a platform-as-a-service provider, providing technical management support, such as IT service management, to the IT operations of large corporations, including providing help desk functionality. The test automation revolves around management of "incident, problem, and change" IT operational events.

  • Leaftaps Opensource ERP Platform

    Opentaps is a web based ERP and CRM for medium sized businesses based on Apache Open for Business. Functionality includes eCommerce, Point-of-Sales, inventory, warehouse, order, customer management and general ledger. The learning and test automation will cover several scenarios around CRM functional tests.

  • Leafground Selenium Playground

    Leafground is a web based application designed for first time selenium webdriver learners. The application has all possible web widgets for automation testing and provides an experience in test automation including the possible challenges that you may have while working on real-time projects.

  • Pynk Travel Platform

    A travel application, like Redbus, that allows users to create a booking with payments, edit their bookings and cancel their services and perform reviews.

  • Chord Large Retail Platform

    A retail platform like amazon with features like search, compare, buy retail items like books, computers, bags, paints and more.
