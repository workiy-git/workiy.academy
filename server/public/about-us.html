About Us





Who we are?

Workiy Academy is a leading software training institute that offers various software courses aligned to the latest industry trends. Our primary aim is to provide quality education and training to students, professionals, and beginners who want to enhance their knowledge and skills in the IT industry.


We believe that the world is constantly evolving, and so are the industries. Therefore, it's crucial to be equipped with the latest knowledge and skills to stay relevant in the market. To ensure this, we have a team of highly experienced and skilled trainers who have vast industry experience and knowledge. They design our courses in a way that helps students learn the practical aspects of the software domain and prepare them for the industry challenges.


At Workiy Academy, we believe in providing practical exposure to our students, and to ensure this, we offer internships to our students. This helps them gain real-time industry experience, and they get a chance to work on live projects. We believe that this hands-on experience prepares them better for industry challenges.


Workiy Academy is the perfect place for anyone who wants to build a successful career in the software domain. We strive to provide the best education and training to our students and help them achieve their goals.

Our Mission

To provide quality education and training in the IT domain that helps students acquire the necessary skills and knowledge to succeed in their careers.


To keep pace with the latest trends and technologies in the IT industry and update their curriculum regularly to ensure that students learn the latest and most relevant skills.


To offer hands-on training to students that helps them gain practical experience and prepare them for real-world challenges.


To provide placement assistance to students and help them secure their dream jobs in top IT companies.


To create a supportive and inclusive learning environment where students can collaborate and learn from each other.


To promote innovation and creativity among students and encourage them to think outside the box and come up with new solutions to real-world problems.

Workiy Academy is the leading Software Training Institute in Chennai which offers the latest and trending courses with a practical learning experience. In addition to the core curriculum, Workiy often offers a range of support services to help students succeed. These may include mentorship programs, career services, and access to a network of alumni and industry professionals.
